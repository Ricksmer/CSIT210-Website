Discover Radisson Blu Hotel

Nestled in the heart of Cebu City, Radisson Blu Cebu provides five-star accommodations with direct access to SM City Cebu Mall, making it the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers. Guests can enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline and Mactan Harbor from elegantly designed rooms equipped with modern amenities, including memory foam beds, high-speed WiFi, and 24-hour room service. The hotel features an outdoor pool, a holistic spa offering traditional Filipino hilot treatments, and a well-equipped fitness center to help guests unwind after a day of exploring.

Conveniently located near historical landmarks such as Magellan’s Cross, Basilica Minore del Sto. Niño, and Fort San Pedro, the hotel also provides easy access to Mango Square’s vibrant nightlife and the pristine white-sand beaches of Cebu. For those looking for adventure, nearby islands and marine sanctuaries offer incredible diving and snorkeling experiences. Business travelers will appreciate the hotel's Executive Lounge, extensive business center, and express services for check-in, check-out, and laundry.

Indulge in world-class dining at Feria Restaurant, which serves an exquisite buffet featuring Filipino and international dishes, or relax with grilled seafood and refreshing cocktails at the Pool Bar. The Lobby Lounge offers afternoon tea, light refreshments, and live nightly entertainment, creating the perfect atmosphere for winding down. Whether you’re in Cebu for a corporate event, a family vacation, or a solo getaway, Radisson Blu Cebu combines luxury, convenience, and warm Filipino hospitality to ensure a memorable stay.
